Mysore

Mysore, located in Karnataka, is a historic city known for its royal heritage and cultural traditions. It was the capital of the Kingdom of Mysore.

The city is famous for the magnificent Mysore Palace, one of the most visited monuments in India.

Mysore is also known for its yoga traditions, sandalwood products, silk sarees, and incense sticks.

The grand Dasara Festival is the highlight of Mysore, attracting tourists with cultural performances and royal processions.

Other attractions include Chamundi Hills, Brindavan Gardens, and St. Philomena’s Church.

The best time to visit Mysore is from October to March.
