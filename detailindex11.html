Ladakh

Ladakh, often called the “Land of High Passes,” is a stunning region located in northern India. It is known for its dramatic landscapes, mountains, and Buddhist monasteries.

The region features high-altitude deserts, snow-capped peaks, and crystal-clear lakes such as Pangong Lake and Tso Moriri.

Ladakh is rich in Tibetan Buddhist culture, with famous monasteries like Hemis, Thiksey, and Diskit.

Adventure activities such as trekking, biking, and river rafting attract thrill-seekers from around the world.

The unique culture, festivals, and peaceful environment make Ladakh a truly unforgettable destination.

The best time to visit Ladakh is from May to September.
