Manali

Manali is a popular hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, known for its scenic beauty, snow-covered mountains, and pleasant climate.

The town lies along the Beas River and is surrounded by pine forests and breathtaking landscapes.

Manali is famous for adventure activities such as paragliding, skiing, trekking, and river rafting.

Popular attractions include Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, Hidimba Devi Temple, and Old Manali.

Manali is also a favorite destination for honeymooners and nature lovers.

The best time to visit Manali is from October to February for snow lovers and March to June for pleasant weather.
