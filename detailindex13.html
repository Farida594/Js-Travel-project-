Madurai

Madurai is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in India, located in Tamil Nadu. It is famous for its ancient temples and rich cultural heritage.

The city is best known for the Meenakshi Amman Temple, a masterpiece of Dravidian architecture with intricately carved towers.

Madurai has been an important center of Tamil culture, literature, and learning for centuries.

The city’s streets, markets, and festivals reflect its deep-rooted traditions and vibrant lifestyle.

Madurai is also known for its traditional South Indian cuisine.

The best time to visit Madurai is from October to March.
