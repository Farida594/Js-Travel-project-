Hampi

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Karnataka and is famous for its ancient ruins and historical significance.

It was once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, one of the richest and most powerful kingdoms in Indian history.

Hampi is known for its impressive temples, stone chariots, royal enclosures, and massive boulders.

The Virupaksha Temple and Vittala Temple are major attractions that showcase remarkable architecture.

The unique landscape and historic atmosphere make Hampi a favorite destination for history lovers.

The best time to visit Hampi is from October to February.
