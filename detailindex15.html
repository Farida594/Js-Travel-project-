Sikkim

Sikkim is a small yet beautiful state in northeastern India, known for its pristine natural beauty and peaceful environment.

The state offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas, including Mount Kanchenjunga, the third-highest peak in the world.

Sikkim is rich in Buddhist culture, with famous monasteries such as Rumtek and Pemayangtse.

Popular tourist spots include Gangtok, Tsomgo Lake, Nathula Pass, and Yumthang Valley.

The state is known for its clean surroundings, eco-friendly tourism, and warm hospitality.

The best time to visit Sikkim is from March to June and September to December.
