Shimla

Shimla is a charming hill station located in Himachal Pradesh and is known for its cool climate, scenic views, and colonial architecture.

Once the summer capital of British India, Shimla still reflects its colonial past through historic buildings and churches.

Popular attractions include The Ridge, Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple, and Christ Church.

Shimla is a popular destination for honeymooners, families, and snow lovers during winter.

The toy train from Kalka to Shimla is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offers breathtaking views.

The best time to visit Shimla is from March to June and December to February for snowfall.
