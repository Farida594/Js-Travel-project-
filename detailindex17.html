Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famously known as the “Pink City” and is rich in royal history and architectural beauty.

The city is home to grand forts and palaces such as Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and Jantar Mantar.

Jaipur is also famous for its colorful bazaars, handicrafts, jewelry, and traditional Rajasthani attire.

The city hosts vibrant festivals and cultural events throughout the year, showcasing Rajasthan’s rich traditions.

Jaipur offers a royal experience combined with warm hospitality and delicious cuisine.

The best time to visit Jaipur is from October to March.
