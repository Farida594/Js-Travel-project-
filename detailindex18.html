Udaipur

Udaipur, also known as the “City of Lakes,” is one of the most romantic cities in India, located in Rajasthan.

The city is famous for its beautiful lakes, palaces, and scenic surroundings.

Major attractions include Lake Pichola, City Palace, Jag Mandir, and Fateh Sagar Lake.

Udaipur is known for its royal heritage, cultural festivals, and traditional art forms.

The peaceful environment and stunning architecture attract visitors from across the world.

The best time to visit Udaipur is from October to March.
