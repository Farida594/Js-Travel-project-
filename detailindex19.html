Bengaluru

Bengaluru, also known as Bangalore, is the capital of Karnataka and is widely recognized as the “Silicon Valley of India.”

The city is a major IT hub with a pleasant climate and modern lifestyle.

Popular attractions include Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Cubbon Park, Bangalore Palace, and Vidhana Soudha.

Bengaluru is known for its vibrant nightlife, cafes, and diverse food culture.

The city blends tradition with innovation, making it a unique destination.

The best time to visit Bengaluru is throughout the year due to its moderate climate.
