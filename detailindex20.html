Mumbai

Mumbai, the capital of Maharashtra, is known as the financial capital of India and the city that never sleeps.

It is home to the Bollywood film industry and offers a fast-paced, vibrant lifestyle.

Major attractions include Gateway of India, Marine Drive, Elephanta Caves, and Juhu Beach.

Mumbai is known for its diverse culture, street food, and historic landmarks.

The city showcases a blend of colonial architecture and modern skyscrapers.

The best time to visit Mumbai is from October to March.
