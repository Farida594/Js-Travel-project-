Kolkata

Kolkata, the capital of West Bengal, is known as the cultural capital of India.

The city is famous for its literature, art, music, and colonial-era architecture.

Iconic landmarks include Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, Indian Museum, and Dakshineswar Kali Temple.

Kolkata is also renowned for its festivals, especially Durga Puja, which is celebrated with great enthusiasm.

The city is known for its delicious sweets like Rasgulla and Sandesh.

The best time to visit Kolkata is from October to February.
