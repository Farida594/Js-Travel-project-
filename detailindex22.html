Pune

Pune is a vibrant city in Maharashtra known for its educational institutions and cultural heritage.

It has a rich history connected to the Maratha Empire and Shivaji Maharaj.

Major attractions include Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, Sinhagad Fort, and Osho Ashram.

Pune is also a growing IT hub with a youthful atmosphere.

The city offers a blend of tradition, modernity, and greenery.

The best time to visit Pune is from October to February.
