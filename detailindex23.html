Tirupati

Tirupati is one of the most important pilgrimage destinations in India, located in Andhra Pradesh.

The city is famous for the Sri Venkateswara Temple situated on Tirumala Hills.

Millions of devotees visit the temple every year seeking blessings.

Tirupati is known for its spiritual atmosphere and religious significance.

The temple is considered one of the richest and most visited religious sites in the world.

The best time to visit Tirupati is from September to March.
