Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is a beautiful hill station in Tamil Nadu, often called the “Princess of Hill Stations.”

It is known for its misty hills, cool climate, and lush greenery.

Popular attractions include Kodaikanal Lake, Coaker’s Walk, Pillar Rocks, and Bryant Park.

Kodaikanal is a favorite destination for honeymooners and nature lovers.

The peaceful environment makes it ideal for relaxation.

The best time to visit Kodaikanal is from April to June and September to October.
