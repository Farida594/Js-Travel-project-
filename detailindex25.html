Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep is a group of beautiful islands located in the Arabian Sea, known for its crystal-clear waters and coral reefs.

It is one of the most pristine and untouched tourist destinations in India.

Lakshadweep is famous for snorkeling, scuba diving, and marine life.

The islands offer a peaceful environment with stunning beaches and turquoise lagoons.

Lakshadweep promotes eco-friendly tourism and limited access to preserve its natural beauty.

The best time to visit Lakshadweep is from October to May.
