Kashmir

Kashmir, often referred to as “Paradise on Earth,” is located in the northern part of India. It is famous for its breathtaking natural beauty, snow-capped mountains, and serene landscapes.

The region is known for its lush valleys, beautiful lakes, and scenic gardens. Dal Lake and Nigeen Lake are popular attractions, offering houseboat stays and shikara rides.

Kashmir is also rich in culture and traditions, known for its handicrafts such as Pashmina shawls, carpets, and papier-mâché items.

Popular tourist destinations include Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Sonmarg. Gulmarg is especially famous for skiing and winter sports.

The region experiences snowfall in winter, making it a favorite destination for snow lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

The best time to visit Kashmir is from March to October, while winter is ideal for experiencing snow and winter sports.
