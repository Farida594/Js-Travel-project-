Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital city of Telangana, is a major cultural and technological hub of India. It is known for its rich history, architecture, and delicious cuisine.

The city has a glorious past under the rule of the Nizams, whose influence can still be seen in historic monuments and traditions.

Famous landmarks include Charminar, Golconda Fort, Chowmahalla Palace, and Hussain Sagar Lake. These monuments showcase the city’s architectural brilliance.

Hyderabad is also known as “Cyberabad” due to its rapid growth in the IT sector, housing major technology companies and startups.

The city is world-famous for Hyderabadi Biryani, along with other traditional dishes that reflect its rich culinary heritage.

The best time to visit Hyderabad is from October to February, when the weather is pleasant for sightseeing.
