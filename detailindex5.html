New Delhi

New Delhi is the capital of India and serves as the political and administrative center of the country. It is known for its wide roads, green spaces, and iconic landmarks.

Designed by British architects Edwin Lutyens and Herbert Baker, New Delhi showcases impressive colonial-era architecture.

Famous attractions include India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhavan, Parliament House, Lotus Temple, and Qutub Minar.

The city is a cultural hub with museums, art galleries, and bustling markets like Connaught Place and Chandni Chowk.

New Delhi also offers a rich culinary experience, from street food to fine dining.

The best time to visit New Delhi is from October to March when the climate is suitable for outdoor exploration.
