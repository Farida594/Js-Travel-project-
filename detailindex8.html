Haridwar

Haridwar is one of the seven holiest cities in India and is located in the state of Uttarakhand. It is considered a gateway to the Himalayas and a major pilgrimage destination.

The city is situated on the banks of the sacred River Ganga and holds great religious significance in Hinduism.

Haridwar is famous for the Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri, where thousands of devotees gather every evening to witness the spiritual ceremony.

The city also hosts the Kumbh Mela, one of the largest religious gatherings in the world.

Apart from its spiritual importance, Haridwar offers a peaceful atmosphere and scenic surroundings.

The best time to visit Haridwar is from October to April, when the weather is pleasant.
