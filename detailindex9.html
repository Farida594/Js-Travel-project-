Kerala

Kerala, known as “God’s Own Country,” is a beautiful state located on the southwestern coast of India. It is famous for its lush greenery, serene backwaters, and scenic landscapes.

The state is renowned for its backwaters in Alleppey and Kumarakom, where houseboat cruises offer a peaceful travel experience.

Kerala is also known for its hill stations like Munnar and Wayanad, which are famous for tea plantations and misty mountains.

The culture of Kerala is rich in classical dance forms such as Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, along with vibrant festivals like Onam and Vishu.

Ayurvedic treatments and wellness tourism are major attractions, drawing visitors from across the world.

The best time to visit Kerala is from October to March when the climate is pleasant.
